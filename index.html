Unfortunately, our experience with Tiniciti Brickell was not a positive one. We chose this daycare because the director who initially welcomed us was very professional and gave us a strong sense of trust. However, just two weeks after our daughter started, we were informed that this person would no longer be at the center. That’s when the issues began.

The care our daughter received changed noticeably, and when we requested a meeting, we were attended by the owner, who had taken over the role of director. During that conversation, it was clear that we were not speaking with a professional in early childhood education, but rather with someone focused on protecting her business interests.

At the beginning, we were asked to purchase books, materials, and painting aprons, but we rarely saw these items being used. When we decided to withdraw our daughter from the center, none of these materials were returned.

Another major concern was the high staff turnover. It’s evident that the center is going through a period of instability and lacks qualified personnel. Although the teachers were kind to our daughter, she came home with diaper rashes on several occasions. This issue persisted for two weeks, and we had to insist that more attention be paid to it. It didn’t seem to be due to a lack of interest, but rather a lack of sufficient staff.

In summary, I would not recommend Tiniciti Brickell at this time. The lack of continuity in the team, poor communication, and lack of transparency regarding materials make it an unsuitable environment for child care.
